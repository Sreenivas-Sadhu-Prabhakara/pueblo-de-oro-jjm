# Pueblo de Oro Website Masterplan

## Project Overview

Pueblo de Oro is a premium residential development in La Aldea Fernandina II, Pampanga, Philippines. This website serves as the primary marketing and lead generation tool for the project.

## Current Status: ✅ COMPLETED

- **Development Phase**: Complete (February 2026)
- **Technical Implementation**: Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS v4
- **Build Status**: Production-ready, no errors
- **Features**: All MVP requirements implemented
- **Deployment**: Ready for production hosting

## Business Goals

- Increase sales by 50X through website traffic and Facebook integration
- Establish Pueblo de Oro as a premium housing brand
- Generate qualified leads through effective marketing funnel
- Convert website visitors into property reservations

## Target Audience

- Young professionals and families (25-45 years old)
- Middle-income households seeking affordable luxury
- Pag-IBIG members looking for financed housing
- Investors interested in appreciating assets

## Key Success Metrics

- Website traffic: 10,000+ monthly visitors
- Lead conversion rate: 5%+
- Average session duration: 3+ minutes
- Mobile traffic: 70%+
- Facebook engagement: High click-through rates

## Website Architecture

- **Landing Page**: Hero section with compelling CTA
- **About Section**: Project overview and value proposition
- **House Models**: Interactive gallery with pricing
- **Location**: Map and proximity benefits
- **Amenities**: Community features showcase
- **Testimonials**: Social proof from homeowners
- **Loan Calculator**: Interactive financing tool
- **FAQ**: Address common concerns
- **Contact Form**: Lead capture with multiple channels

## Marketing Funnel

1. **Awareness**: Facebook ads driving traffic
2. **Interest**: Engaging content and visuals
3. **Consideration**: Detailed information and calculator
4. **Intent**: Contact form and consultation booking
5. **Purchase**: Reservation and financing process
